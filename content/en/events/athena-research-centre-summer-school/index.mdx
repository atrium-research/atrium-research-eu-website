ATRIUM participated in the Summer Institute: Digital Humanities for Hellenic Studies, which focused on "Visualizing the Past: Mapping Athens' Lost Neighborhood." This summer school, organized by the Athena Research Centre, provided training in digital methods for understanding and reconstructing historical urban landscapes, aligning closely with ATRIUM's mission to advance frontier research in the arts and humanities through innovative digital approaches.
