At the Interspeech Conference 2025, one of the premier international conferences on speech science and technology, ATRIUM presented "Transcribing Oral History Recordings Using the Transcription Portal." This presentation showcased how ATRIUM's infrastructure supports researchers working with audio data, demonstrating the practical applications of the CLARIN Transcription Portal for scholars in the humanities. The presentation highlighted how automated speech recognition technologies can facilitate access to oral history collections and other audio-based research materials.
