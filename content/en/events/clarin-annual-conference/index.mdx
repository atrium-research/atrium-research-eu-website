CLARIN is the European research infrastructure that makes digital language resources available to scholars, researchers, students and citizen scientists from a wide range of disciplines, coordinates the collection of language resources and tools, and offers advanced tools to explore, exploit, annotate, analyse or combine such datasets, regardless of their location. The CLARIN Conference 2025 will be a face-to-face event that will be fully accessible virtually. The conference will take place in Vienna, Austria. The event will be hosted and organised by CLARIN ERIC in collaboration with CLARIAH-AT.&#x20;
