ATRIUM presented at the Digital Heritage International Congress 2025, one of the leading international forums for discussing the digitization, preservation, and accessibility of cultural heritage. The presentation focused on "Hypothetical Reconstruction for the Conservation, Preservation and Valorisation of Cultural Heritage: the Kampanopetra Basilica in Salamis, Cyprus." This work demonstrated how digital reconstruction techniques can contribute to understanding and preserving endangered archaeological sites, showcasing the practical applications of ATRIUM's workflows and methodologies.
