tourismA 2025 will take place in **Florence from 23 to 25 February**, in the Palazzo dei Congressi. A moment to **display, disseminate and talk** about all the communication activities of the ancient world and the enhancement of its artefacts.

The event is aimed at all cultural and economic organizations of the archaeological-artistic-monumental sector: public and private research institutions, parks, museums, tour operators, and all professional categories.

During the three days the MiC will be present with an institutional exhibition space, curated by Service VI of the General Secretariat, dedicated to the Ministry's initiatives in the field of archeology and recent discoveries, in particular the Bronzes of San Casciano.
