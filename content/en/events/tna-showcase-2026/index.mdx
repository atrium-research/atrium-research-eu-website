Want to learn more about the ATRIUM transnational access (TNA) research grant scheme? Join us for the second annual ATRIUM TNA Showcase, which features recipients of the scheme who have completed summer schools and individual access placements across Europe.

The showcase will take place on 14th April at 11:00 CET and will last approximately one hour.&#x20;

\
TNA participants, prospective applicants, and the wider ATRIUM community are all invited to learn about project outcomes, the different placements available, and to bring any questions they might have about the scheme. 

\
We are excited to feature Eric Okoyo (British Institute in Eastern Africa), Martha Mosha (University of Cologne), Daniel Kansaon (Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais) and Dr Nicky Garland (Archaeology Data Service).&#x20;
