ATRIUM Principle Investigator, Toma Tasovac, was invited to speak at the COAR event **Publish-Review-Curate: Turning Scholarly Publishing on Its Head** in Cambridge.&#x20;

Toma presented ATRIUM's ongoing work on the assessment of non-traditional research outputs and how they are concretely used in the Overlay journal *Transformations: A DARIAH Journal.*&#x20;

### PRC as a Model for Rethinking Scholarly Publishing in the Arts and Humanities

Publish-Review-Curate **(**&#x50;RC) is emerging as a key point of convergence for diamond open access, open peer review, research-assessment reform, and public research infrastructures.&#x20;
