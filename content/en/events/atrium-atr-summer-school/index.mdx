Automatic Text Recognition (ATR) has become key to unlocking information from scanned documents containing text and is now used in Cultural Heritage Research worldwide. The ATRIUM ATR Summer School brings together Digital Humanities practitioners willing to improve their Automatic Text Recognition pipeline. With a focus on novel methodologies, complex workflows and Open Science implementation, it is a unique opportunity for participants to take advantage of the expertise of the international trainer team to strengthen and level up their ATR project.
