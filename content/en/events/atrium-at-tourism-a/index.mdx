ATRIUM will take part in tourismA 2026, one of Italy’s most important international events dedicated to archaeology, cultural heritage, and the communication of the ancient world. Held every year in Florence at the prestigious Palazzo dei Congressi, tourismA is a three-day event that brings together museums, research institutions, universities, cultural associations, heritage professionals, tourism operators, and innovators.

As part of the programme, ATRIUM will organise a dedicated conference session and will also be present with a joint stand together with the <Link link={{"discriminant":"external","value":"https://www.artemis-twin.eu/"}}>ARTEMIS project.</Link>
