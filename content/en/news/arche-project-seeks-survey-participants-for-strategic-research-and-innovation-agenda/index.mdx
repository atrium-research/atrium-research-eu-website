The EU-funded ARCHE Project (Alliance for Research on Cultural Heritage in Europe), on behalf of DG RTD D4 (Democracy, Equality and Culture), invites participants to respond to the consultation on the [Key Messages and Preliminary Findings](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/www.heritageresearch-hub.eu/app/uploads/2024/09/ARCHE-SRIA-Key-messages-and-preliminary-findings.pdf__;!!DOxrgLBm!BQm5T-9xalddcUQ1Pu0eeB5x-AG1QiiQ4K1GJsBsKuBS8jFuvu3-3kRdnIhJugow55bC0bkTFgCNM1MO76-hcPkWl256ZjwvPfBCwTTEPA$) of the upcoming Strategic Research and Innovation Agenda (SRIA) for the proposed co-funded **European Partnership on Resilient Cultural Heritage.**&#x20;

The Partnership proposal can be found at [European Partnerships under the Horizon Europe strategic plan 2025-2027 - European Commission (europa.eu)](https://research-and-innovation.ec.europa.eu/funding/funding-opportunities/funding-programmes-and-open-calls/horizon-europe/european-partnerships-horizon-europe/european-partnerships-under-horizon-europe-strategic-plan-2025-2027_en)

Your feedback, to be provided by **15 November 2024**, will be essential in refining the SRIA as developed by ARCHE. You can send your ideas via the Consultation survey, available at [https://ec.europa.eu/eusurvey/runner/ARCHE\_SRIA\_Stakeholders\_Consultation](https://ec.europa.eu/eusurvey/runner/ARCHE_SRIA_Stakeholders_Consultation).
