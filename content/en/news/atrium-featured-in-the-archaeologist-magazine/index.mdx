ATRIUM is pleased to be featured in the latest edition of *The Archaeologist*, with the article "Advanced Digital Workflows for Archaeology", written by Émilie Pagé-Perron (Archaeology Data Service) and Julian Richards (Archaeology Data Service and Heritage Science Data Service). *The Archaeologist,* CIfA’s magazine, covers important topics for professional archaeologists in the UK and anyone interested in professional archaeology. Published three times a year in full colour, the magazine includes feature articles covering new research, techniques and practice about different parts of the profession, member news celebrating the successes and achievements of members and their work short articles updating on the work of the Institute through our committees, volunteers and strategic aims.&#x20;

Visit the [website for the Chartered Institute of Archaeologists]([https://www.archaeologists.net/publications/the-archaeologist]\(https://www.archaeologists.net/publications/the-archaeologist\)) to learn more. [  ](https://www.archaeologists.net/publications/the-archaeologist)
